About the Project

Egypt's Finest
Mediterranean Resort

Seashore by Hyde Park Developments spans 240 magnificent acres along the Mediterranean coastline of Ras El Hekma. Masterplanned by EDSA and SB Architects of Florida, the resort fuses avant-garde architecture with the natural splendour of the North Coast — turquoise waters, palm-lined promenades and vast open greenery. With 1,850 residential units and 90% direct sea views, Seashore is not just a resort — it's a permanent escape.
