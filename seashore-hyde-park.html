About Seashore

Egypt's Premier
Mediterranean Resort

Seashore by Hyde Park Developments is a world-class coastal resort on the Mediterranean Sea in Ras El Hekma, North Coast. Designed by globally renowned architects EDSA and SB Architects of Florida, the resort blends avant-garde architecture with the emerald waters and lush greenery of Egypt's finest coastline. With 90% of units enjoying direct sea views, Seashore is not just a resort – it's a lifestyle.
